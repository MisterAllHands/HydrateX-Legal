## Introduction

Hydrate-X ("we," "our," or "the app") is a hydration tracking application designed to help you monitor your daily water intake and maintain healthy hydration habits. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When you use Hydrate-X, you may provide the following information:

- **Account Information:** Email address (when creating an account or signing in)
- **Profile Information:** Name, gender, age, height, and weight
- **Health & Fitness Data:** Daily water intake logs, beverage preferences, weight tracking entries, and hydration goals

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Identifiers:** Anonymous user ID for authentication and subscription management
- **Purchase Information:** Subscription status (processed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Location data
- Contacts or address book
- Photos (unless you choose to set a profile photo, which is stored locally only)
- Browsing history
- Third-party advertising identifiers

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

1. **Provide App Functionality:** Calculate personalized hydration goals based on your profile (weight, age, activity level)
2. **Track Your Progress:** Store your water intake history and weight tracking data
3. **Manage Your Account:** Enable sign-in across devices using Firebase Authentication
4. **Process Subscriptions:** Verify premium subscription status through RevenueCat
5. **Send Notifications:** Deliver hydration reminders (only if you enable them)

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Most of your data (profile, water entries, preferences) is stored **locally on your device**
- Local data is encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption
- Encryption keys are stored securely in the iOS Keychain

### Cloud Services
- **Firebase Authentication:** Your email and authentication tokens are processed by Google Firebase to enable account sign-in
- **RevenueCat:** Your anonymous user ID and subscription status are processed to manage premium features

### Data Retention
- Your data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data
- Account data on Firebase is retained until you delete your account
- You can delete your account at any time through the app settings

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access Your Data:** View all your data within the app
- **Export Your Data:** Export your hydration history from the app settings
- **Delete Your Data:** Delete your account and all associated data
- **Opt Out of Notifications:** Disable reminders in app settings or iOS settings
- **Use Without Account:** Use the app's core features without creating an account
